Publishing Instructions

 

 

Accepted papers and symposia will be published in a forthcoming volume of Philosophy of Science.  The deadline for the final version of the papers is January 15, 2005, and for the symposia is September 1, 2005.  Manuscripts should be prepared in Philosophy of Science house style (see Instructions for Authors at http://www.indiana.edu/~philsci ) in Word (preferably) or TeX plus PDF. 

Paper length is limited to 5000 words including notes and bibliography.

 

 

 

Paper, symposia and workshop contributions may all be archived at the PhilSci Archive.  For papers, this should be done as soon as possible.  Instructions are at http://www.pitt.edu/~hpsdept/resources/PhilSci-Archive_Instructions.htm

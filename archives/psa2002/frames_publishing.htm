Accepted Symposia Papers

Symposia Papers accepted for consideration for publication in Philosophy of Science must be submitted directly to the Program Chair by January 15, 2003.

Symposia Papers may be deposited in the Phil-Sci Archive at any time.  You will have to register with the archive in order to deposit your paper.  Make sure that the paper is listed under the "Conferences and Volumes: Philosophy of Science Assoc. 18th Biennial Mtg - PSA 2002: Symposia" subject heading.  You may list the paper under additional subject headings if you wish.  Retain your registration and paper information, as you will be able to update your paper after it is deposited.

 

Accepted Workshop Papers

Workshop Papers may be deposited in the Phil-Sci Archive at any time.  You will have to register with the archive in order to deposit your paper.  Make sure that the paper is listed under the "Conferences and Volumes: Philosophy of Science Assoc. 18th Biennial Mtg - PSA 2002: Workshops" subject heading.  You may list the paper under additional subject headings if you wish.  Retain your registration and paper information, as you will be able to update your paper after it is deposited.

 

Accepted Contributed Papers

Accepted papers will be reviewed for publication in Philosophy of Science and deposited in the Phil-Sci Archive by the Program Committee.  Final versions must be submitted directly to the Program Chair by January 15, 2003.

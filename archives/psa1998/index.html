PSA 98 was held 22-24 Oct 1998 in Kansas City, Missouri at the Hyatt Regency Hotel, in conjunction with the annual meeting of the History of Science Society. 

Many conference participants visited Linda Hall Library, which has one of the world's finest collections of science, engineering, and technology materials.

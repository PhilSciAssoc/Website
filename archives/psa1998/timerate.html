Schedule (preliminary information)

PSA'98 commences on Thursday, October 22 at 9:00am with registration and the opening of the book exhibit. Attendees are advised that it is worthwhile to arrive at the hotel on Wednesday if they wish, since there will be a block of rooms available for Wednesday night.

Session times are as follows:

    Thur 1:00 - 3:30; 4:00 - 6:30
    Fri  9:00 - 11:30; 1:00 - 3:30; 4:00 - 6:30
    Sat 9:00 - 11:30; 1:00 - 3:30

The meeting will end when the last session of Saturday ends, or when the last glass is emptied at the reception Saturday evening.

The complete program for PSA'98 will be posted here soon.
